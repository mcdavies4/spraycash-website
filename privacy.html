Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At SprayCash, operated by The 36th Company Ltd, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. By using SprayCash, you agree to the practices described here.

📋 Summary: We collect only what we need to operate the app. We never sell your personal data. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@spraycash.app.

1. Who We Are

SprayCash is a digital money-spraying application operated by The 36th Company Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales. Our registered address is in London, United Kingdom.

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at privacy@spraycash.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password when you register
  • Identity verification — government-issued ID for KYC (Know Your Customer) compliance above certain transaction limits
  • Financial data — wallet balance, transaction history, top-up records
  • Device information — device type, operating system, and NFC capability for app functionality
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, screens visited, features used
  • Location data — approximate location only when required for fraud prevention (not continuously tracked)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your SprayCash account
  • Process payments and wallet transactions
  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Comply with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations
  • Send you transaction notifications and account updates
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns
  • Respond to customer support requests

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

4. Payment Processing

SprayCash uses Flutterwave (for Nigerian Naira transactions) and Stripe (for UK Pound transactions) to process payments. These providers are regulated financial institutions and have their own privacy policies:

  • Flutterwave Privacy Policy
  • Stripe Privacy Policy

We do not store your full card details. All payment data is handled directly by our regulated payment partners.

5. Data Sharing

We only share your data with:

  • Payment processors — Flutterwave and Stripe, to process transactions
  • Identity verification providers — for KYC compliance where required
  • Regulatory authorities — HMRC, FCA, or CBN if required by law
  • Cloud infrastructure — Supabase for secure database storage

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or marketing companies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by UK financial regulations. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Object — object to certain uses of your data
  • Withdraw consent — at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@spraycash.app. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We protect your data using industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS), encrypted storage, and access controls. NFC transactions use short-lived signed tokens — even if intercepted, they cannot be reused. However, no system is 100% secure and we encourage you to use a strong password and keep your device secure.

9. Children's Privacy

SprayCash is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notification. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

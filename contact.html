Frequently asked questions

How does NFC money spraying work? +
Both phones need to have SprayCash open. Hold the backs of the phones close together — NFC connects them automatically. The sender taps the screen to spray money. Each tap sends one denomination. The money moves instantly between wallets.
Is SprayCash safe to use? +
Yes. Every NFC transaction uses a short-lived signed token from our servers. Even if intercepted, the token expires in seconds and cannot be reused. Payments are processed by Flutterwave and Stripe — both regulated financial institutions.
Can I get a refund if I spray the wrong person? +
Transactions are final once confirmed, as stated in our Terms of Service. However, if you believe you've been defrauded or there was a technical error, contact us immediately at support@spraycash.app and we'll investigate.
What currencies does SprayCash support? +
Currently SprayCash supports Nigerian Naira (NGN) via Flutterwave. British Pound (GBP) support via Stripe is coming soon for UK-based users.
Is there a transaction limit? +
Unverified accounts have lower transaction limits. Completing KYC identity verification increases your limits significantly. Details are shown in the app under your profile settings.
When is the iOS version coming? +
We're working on the iOS version and expect to launch on the App Store in 2026. Register your interest by emailing support@spraycash.app and we'll notify you the moment it's live.

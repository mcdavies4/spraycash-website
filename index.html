How it works

Simple as a tap.
Magical as the moment.

No cash. No mess. Just pure celebration energy transferred phone to phone.

01
👛

Load Your Wallet

Top up your SprayCash wallet via bank transfer, debit card, or USSD. Your money is secure and ready to spray.

02
💰

Pick Your Note

Choose your denomination — ₦200, ₦500, ₦1,000 or ₦5,000. Each tap sends that amount flying.

03
📡

Tap & Spray

Hold your phone near the recipient's phone. NFC connects instantly. Tap the screen and watch the money fly.

04
🤑

Receive Instantly

Money lands in the recipient's wallet immediately. No delays, no bank transfers, no waiting.

Features

Everything you need
to spray right.

📡

NFC Tap-to-Pay

Real peer-to-peer money transfer via NFC. No QR codes, no usernames — just tap and spray. Works on all modern Android and iPhone devices.

💸

Bill Animations

Digital notes fly off your screen in real time. The experience feels exactly like real spraying — just cleaner, faster and safer.

👛

Smart Wallet

Top up via bank transfer, debit card or USSD. View your full transaction history — every spray sent and received, in one place.

⚡

Instant Settlement

Powered by Flutterwave. Money moves instantly between wallets. No delays, no failed transactions at the most important moments.

🔒

Secure by Design

Every transaction uses a short-lived signed token. Even if someone intercepts the NFC signal, they can't steal your money.

🌍

Built for Diaspora

Designed for Nigerians and Africans in the UK and beyond. Supports both NGN and GBP. Works at any event, anywhere in the world.
